# school_read_levels

Physical literacy and reading performance of amazonian school children.

### Description

This is a dataset from an association, cross-sectional, quantitative
study carried out with Amazonian schoolchildren (n=100; aged between 10
and 13 years) from 3 public schools in the municipality of Borba
(Amazonas).

### Usage

```r
school_read_levels
```

### Format

‘school_read_levels’ A data frame with 100 rows and 17 columns:

#### school

School of observation

#### sex

Sex - 1 for Masculine and 2 for Feminine

#### age

Age (in years)

#### weight_kg

Weight in kilograms (kg)

#### height_m

Height in meters (m)

#### bmi

Body Mass Index (BMI)

#### bmi_status

Body Mass Index Status - A categorical description of the BMI.

#### phv

Peak Height Velocity (PHV) is a continuous variable, measured by age (in
years) relative to the peak height in the period where maximum rate of
growth occurs and this is an indication of maturity.

#### phv_state

Categorical description for Peak Height Velocity (PHV).

#### eses

Exercise Self-Efficacy Scale measures how confident the students are
with regard to carrying out regular physical activities and exercise,
measured by a psychometric scale.

#### pmc

Perceived Motor Competence (PMC) assesses the child’s self-perception
regarding their ability to perform motor movements. Measured by a
psychometric scale.

#### kupa

Knowledge and Understanding of Physical Activity (KUPA) - Evaluates
knowledge regarding the benefits, safety, and engagement in physical
activity. Measured by scores obtained in objective questions.

#### sb

Sedentary Behavior - Evalutes the exposition time to sedentary
behaviors. Measured by psychometric scale.

#### pa

Pyshical Activity (PA) - Level of physical activity in children, based
on the frequency and duration of routine daily activities. Measured by
score on the physical activity frequency questions.

#### sts_s

Supine To Stand (in seconds) - Measures the time (in seconds) it takes
for the child to stand up from the floor, from a supine position to a
standing position.

#### tde

Total Score on the TDE test - Measures reading level based on time and
accuracy in word reading.

#### tde_categorical

Categorical description of the TDE Score.
